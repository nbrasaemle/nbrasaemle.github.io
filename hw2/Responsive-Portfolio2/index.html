About Me


I am currently enrolled in the University of Minnesota's Full-Stack Web Development Coding Boot Camp. In this boot camp I am learning to become a Full-Stack Web Developer. I have experience in sales and managing. I currently work in retail. I have had some programming experience in Java, prior to this boot camp, and I have enjoyed the boot camp so far. I hope to work with computers, programming, or web development when finished with this boot camp.


When I am not coding, I like to spend time with my family and our dogs, go hiking at local and state parks, and read. I also enjoy watching movies, tv, and playing video games for the PC, Wii, Xbox 360, and Nintendo DS. My absolute favorite game is "The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim" made by Bethsaida; my favorite movie series is "The Lord of the Rings", specifically "The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers". I also have a passion for ceramics and pottery, painting, knitting, and crocheting.
