What supplies do I need to have before I own a dog?

After you have decided what type of dog to own, some basic supplies include:

  • Leash and Collar
  • ID Tag, all current information
  • Dog friendly toys
  • Dog food fit for your pet
  • Dog bedding
  • Water and food bowls, preferrably stainless steel

For detailed information, checkout the Community Resources page.

Which vaccines does my new dog need?

Make sure your dog has at the following vaccinations at minimum:

  • Rabies Virus
  • Canin Distempter Virus
  • Canine Adenovirus-2 (Hepatitus)
  • Canine Parvovirus

For more information about the vaccines, checkout the Community Resources page.

Which vet clinic should I visit with my new dog?

There are many factors to consider when choosing a vet clinic. There are shelters that offer clinic services and are non-profit, as well as for-profit clinics; depending on the needs of your dog and your budget, you might want to gather more information from the shelter you adopted your dog from.


Find Vet Clinics
What type of questions should I ask the shelter about the dog I choose?

As a new pet owner of the dog you choose, you might want to ask key questions to get an idea if the dog you found near your area is the right fit for you. Some questions include:

  • How long has the pet been in this shelter?
  • Has this pet been adopted before? If yes, why was it returned?
  • Are there any behavioral issues I should be aware of before I adopt it?
  • Does this dog have any training?
  • If the dog cannot adapt to its new home, can I return it to the shelter?

Check the Community Resources page if you have more questions.
